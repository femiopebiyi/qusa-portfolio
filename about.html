Company overview

Driven by quality,
powered by expertise

QUSA Auto Shop & Trading is a premium automotive service and maintenance facility, established in 2023 and located in Al Wukair, Doha, Qatar.

Founded by Mohammed Abdul Azeez and Nimal Wijerathna, QUSA was established to address the growing demand for quality workmanship, transparency, availability and trustworthy automotive service within the local market.

With over 20 years of combined industry experience, our management team has built QUSA around technical excellence, customer satisfaction and long-term relationships.

Today, QUSA provides comprehensive automotive solutions for European, American, Japanese, luxury, performance and off-road vehicles, through a modern workshop equipped with advanced diagnostic technology and highly skilled technicians.
